OUR GOALS

Understand the reasons for GYST


As high school students, we understand how stressful it may be to find scholarships for college. The process for each scholarship application is usually long and tricky. Often students have a hard time keeping track of all the scholarships they are applying to. Rushing to reach


Here at GYST, we hope to create a place where all high school students can organize their college scholarship information. Students can also learn about the various scholarships through recommended websites. More often than not, students get anxious about whether they will be able to cover the cost of a college education. Students from underpriviledged backgrounds may not have enough resources to seek guidance about the process. Whatever type of student you may be, we hope this website will provide you with helpful resources, advice, and a community to share your experiene with others.
